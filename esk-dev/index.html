About

Our mission statement: The Epiphany Soup Kitchen strives to provide hearty, nutritious meals, while fostering a sense of community, one plate at a time.

S. Stephen's Church is host to the Epiphany Soup Kitchen, an independent nonprofit corporation, which serves people in need from Providence, RI and surrounding areas.

Epiphany Soup Kitchen is a long-established institution that was originally housed in south Providence by the now-defunct Church of the Epiphany. From there, the ESK went to Grace Church downtown, where they provided this much-needed service for several years. S. Stephen's has hosted ESK since October of 2009. ESK is staffed weekly by volunteers from different parishes and organizations. Extra hands are always welcome. Feel free to come and help!

For Guests

On Saturdays from 3:30 to 5 pm, all are welcome to enjoy a hot meal in the Great Hall of our Guild House (to the left of the worship hall; enter through the red double doors and go up a flight of stairs). We also occasionally offer free, clean, gently-used clothing to our guests.

Besides the entrees, we also provide bottled water, coffee, bread, fresh fruit, and salad. Come join us for a hot meal!

For Volunteers

We cannot do this without the help of our wonderful volunteers (ages 12 and up), so please sign up to donate your time to this worthy cause! No experience is necessary, and an ESK Board member will be present each week to assist in directing volunteers.

It is required that all volunteers are masked and gloved while handling food in order to help out at the Epiphany Soup Kitchen. This is for the safety of the vulnerable population we serve, as well as each other! Unwillingness to comply will result in the inability to volunteer at ESK.

You can sign up for shifts at this link. Scroll down to the bottom for future dates.

If you have any questions or need assistance signing up, please email our volunteer coordinator, Elizabeth, at ESK.Prov@gmail.com!
