Our Chapter




Collierville High School TSA (CHS TSA) was founded in 2014 and has inspired members to pursue the infinite possibilities in the realm of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

The Motto for the 2019-2020 Collierville High School TSA Chapter:
“Looking forward to the future, because we are it!”

Creed: Students are expected to participate in a variety of competitions, fundraisers, and informational sessions to continue the legacy of Collierville High School TSA to the best of their ability. Members are responsible for paying all fees and dues for conferences. Proper TSA attire must be worn by members at competitions, and the rules set in place by TSA, the advisors, and the school are to be followed at all times.

Collierville High School TSA encourages students to attend the regional and state conferences in events that interest them, whether it may be biotechnology or children’s stories. In addition, we motivate members to participate in events they may not be as educated about, for we strive to widen their horizons on the reach of STEM in modern society.
